# Red Door Luxe — Two-Page Architecture Strategy

This document outlines the new two-page structure for the Red Door Luxe website. The goal is to separate the **business pitch to the owner** from the **live product demonstration**.

This creates two distinct, focused experiences:
1.  `index.html`: A persuasive landing page for the salon suite owner (Dolli).
2.  `directory.html`: A standalone, fully functional directory page that serves as the product demo.

---

## Page 1: `index.html` — The Owner-Facing Pitch

This page is a linear, persuasive narrative designed to walk a salon suite owner from their primary business problem to our clear, priced solution.

**Audience:** Salon Suite Owner (Dolli Bell-Lewis)
**Goal:** Convince her to purchase the Professional Directory System.

### Narrative Flow:

| Section | Title | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Hero** | **"One Empty Suite Costs You $1,500/Month. This System Fills It."** | Immediately frame the problem in terms of lost revenue and position the directory as the solution. The primary CTA will be "See the Solution". |
| **1. The Problem** | **Why Good Tenants Leave** | Detail the pain point: tenant turnover due to lack of client acquisition. Use the market stats (7,000+ searches, 83% on Google) to show that Instagram isn't enough. |
| **2. The Solution** | **A Professional Directory That Works For You** | Introduce the directory system as the core solution. Explain that it's not just a list of names, but a client acquisition and retention tool. |
| **3. Why It Works** | **How This Directory Gets Your Tenants Clients** | Explain the mechanics: Google SEO, AI search optimization, and direct booking links. Show *how* the directory translates into real-world visibility and bookings. |
| **4. The Result** | **Higher Retention, Faster Leasing, More Revenue** | Present the tangible ROI. Use the math from the playbook: one retained tenant pays for the system 2-4x over. This section proves the financial value. |
| **5. The Outreach** | **We Find Your Next Tenant For You** | Introduce the tenant acquisition service. This is a powerful new section that shows we don't just support current tenants—we help her grow. We will feature the "Prospective Professional" lead examples here to make it concrete. |
| **6. Pricing** | **Simple, Flat-Rate Investment** | The three-tier pricing table (Starter, Professional, Growth). This is the clear, final offer. |
| **7. CTA** | **Schedule a 15-Minute Call** | A final, direct call to action to get on the phone and close the deal. |

---

## Page 2: `directory.html` — The Standalone Product

This page is the live, functioning directory. It has one job: to look incredible and work perfectly. It will be linked from the owner-facing page as the "live demo."

**Audience:** Prospective Clients & Prospective Tenants
**Goal:** Impress visitors and prove the directory is a high-quality, valuable product.

### Structure:

| Section | Content | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Header** | Simple Red Door Luxe logo and a link back to the owner page. | Clean navigation. |
| **Filter Bar** | The filter buttons: All, Hair, Nails, Lashes, etc. | Demonstrate the core filtering functionality. |
| **Professional Grid** | The four main professional cards (Gena, Tina, Maria, Marcus). | Show the directory in action with real, established tenants. |
| **Lead Examples** | **"Prospective Professionals We're Reaching Out To"** | A new grid section showing 2-3 example lead profiles (e.g., Jasmine the hair stylist). Each card will have a "Claim This Profile" button. This makes the outreach system tangible and demonstrates the value of the higher-tier packages. |
| **Footer** | Simple footer with contact info. | |

This two-page structure allows us to tell a compelling story to the business owner while simultaneously providing a clean, impressive showcase of the product itself. It separates the "why" from the "what," creating a much clearer and more effective user experience.
